Augustus Arntz

Broker

Augustus followed his father Julius' footsteps into the real estate business right out of college. Father and son worked together at another realtor for a few years before Julius founded Tennessee Valley Realty in 1985, with Augustus joining shortly thereafter. Julius retired in 2003 and Augustus has been at the helm ever since. Augustus takes a special pleasure in finding the right home for young families.

Outside of work, his hobbies include woodworking and playing with his grandchildren.
